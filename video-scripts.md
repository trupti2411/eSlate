## Recording Tips

1. **Screen Resolution:** Record at 1920x1080 for best quality
2. **Mouse Movements:** Move cursor slowly and deliberately
3. **Transitions:** Pause 1-2 seconds between actions
4. **Clean Data:** Use realistic but fictional student/tutor names
5. **E-ink Demo:** If possible, record actual e-ink tablet usage
6. **Music:** Add subtle background music in editing
7. **Timing:** Each scene should be 5-10 seconds
8. **Total Length:** Aim for 1:15 - 1:50 per video
